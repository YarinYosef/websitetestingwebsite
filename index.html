vaultbridge

for higher consistent yield. the vaultbridge redeploys certain bridged assets, like ETH, USDC, and more, into yield-bearing strategies on ethereum. revenue cycles back to katana for high consistent yield on specific DEX LPs and lending pools

chain-owned liquidity (CoL)

creates deep liquidity for core defi primitives. katana owns this core liquidity reserve, replenished via 100% of net sequencer fees and a cut of core app revenue. CoL reduces slippage, provides predictable borrowing rates, and acts as a shock absorber in volatile markets

core apps

katana is opinionated. CoL and vaultbridge yield flows to core apps – one per defi primitive – to concentrate liquidity and boost yield. these are defi leaders such as morpho and sushi. a cut of their revenue flows back to the network through CoL and targeted real yield incentives

AUSD

katana's native stablecoin, backed by offchain US treasuries. AUSD is issued by agora, the reserves are custodied by state street and managed by vaneck. AUSD brings offchain yield onchain, routed directly to katana. supports liquidity incentives across AUSD-denominated lending markets and DEX liquidity pools for diversified offchain yield incentives.

vbTokens

users receive 1:1 wrapped vbTokens of certain bridged assets. behind the scenes, bags go to work in low-risk defi strategies on ethereum, generating real yield. on katana, vbTokens unlock boosted rewards when actively deployed in core applications. holding vbTokens passively does not accrue yield, they must be active
